Heritage Research

There are various events affecting, studies done, and even scope of what constitutes being mixed race in South Africa. This is the corner where you can learn, be directed to further resources or view the author's current heritage related projects.

Family History

My own family history serves not only as a case study for the heritage research, but also a place of inspiration for future generations and current relatives to access information easier as mixed race history was not as well kept. Also find the discoveries of forgotten history, poetry and prose, and realisation of identity based on evidence and the journey thoughts along the way.
